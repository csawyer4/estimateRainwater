Calculating Yearly
Rainwater Collection

Use the map to draw a polygon over your roof to calculate the amount of water you could collect in one year. The precipitation data comes from King County and is the average of yearly totals going back to 2014. Due to this you will not get an accurate estimate if you reside outside of King County Boundaries.


You Could Collect How Many Gallons of Rainwater?

How the Tool Works

This is a leaflet map that geolocates the user. This allows the user to swiftly find their home. A leaflet-measure plugin gives the map the capability to draw a polygon and display the area in square feet. Another plugin, turf.js, is used to get the nearest rain gauge station's precipitation to the users location. When the polygon is finished the area and precipitation are multiplied, converted, and formatted to display gallons of rainwater that one could collect in a year.
